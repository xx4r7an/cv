Itachi Uchiha

Member of Uchiha clan.

Itachi Uchiha (うちはイタチ, Uchiha Itachi) was a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan who served as an Anbu Captain.

Appearance

  • Itachi was a fairly tall man of fair complexion.
  • He had black eyes.
  • He had jet-black hair that was pulled back in a low ponytail and his face was framed with centre-parted bangs that extended to his chin
